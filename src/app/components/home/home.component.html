About

TaskNote Flow unites your to-dos and your thinking in one fast, tidy workspace. Instead of juggling separate apps, capture ideas, turn them into tasks, and track progress from a single, calm interface.

Built by productivity nerds who value speed and privacy, TaskNote Flow is keyboard-first, offline-capable, and syncs securely across devices so your work is always accessible.

  • Local-first with optional encrypted cloud sync
  • Keyboard-first commands for rapid capture and organization
  • Works across iOS, Android, Web, and Desktop

FAQ

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The Free plan includes unlimited notes and tasks, plus sync across two devices.

 Can I import from other apps?

You can import from Markdown, CSV, Evernote export, Apple Notes (via export), and Todoist CSV.

 Does it work offline?

Yes. Capture and edit offline; changes sync securely once you're back online.

 How is my data secured?

We use encryption at rest and TLS in transit. You can also export your data anytime.

 Do you have mobile apps?

Native apps are available for iOS and Android, plus web and desktop clients.
